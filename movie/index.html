Avatar II: The way of water

As the battle rages on, Jake Sully, now fully immersed in his Na'vi avatar, is confronted by Colonel Miles Quaritch, the leader of the human forces. Quaritch, who has a deep hatred for the Na'vi and all that they represent, attempts to kill Sully and end the conflict once and for all.
However, Sully, aided by his love for Neytiri and his newfound understanding of Pandora and its ways, is able to overcome Quaritch and defeat him. In the process, Sully also helps to unite the various clans of the Na'vi and rally them against the humans, leading to a decisive victory for the indigenous people of Pandora.
